Current Projects

YMCA Racquetball Queue

In my spare time, I am currently building a web-based application for YMCAs in the greater Des Moines Area to allow members to reserve racquetball courts. This will allow members reserve and cancel courts and see peak court usage hours. This will simplify the court reservation from the current process and save time for both members and YMCA staff.
